Frequently asked questions from Quebec dental practices

Local Pack & Visibility

Google's Local Pack is the block of 3 results that appears at the top of search results when a patient searches for "dentist Plateau" or "emergency dentist Montreal". These 3 results capture up to 75 % of all clicks (BrightLocal 2024). If your practice is not in that block, you are invisible to the majority of patients searching right now. Google selects practices for the Local Pack based on relevance, proximity and prominence — three dimensions we optimize concretely for your clinic. Appearing in this block is worth considerably more than having a beautiful website with no visibility.

For practices starting from a low baseline, the first ranking improvements are typically visible within 4 to 8 weeks after Google Business Profile optimization. Reaching a stable top-3 position in a competitive neighbourhood like Plateau or Rosemont generally takes 3 to 6 months of sustained work. Results depend on competition levels in your neighbourhood, your volume of recent reviews, and the quality of your Schema markup. Our audit will give you an honest estimate for your specific situation.

Being on Google Maps is only the first step of a much longer optimization journey. A well-optimized Google Business Profile includes precise categories (Dentist + Orthodontist if applicable), a complete list of services (whitening, implants, emergency care), recent professional photos, weekly posts, and — most importantly — a steady stream of recent reviews with responses. The majority of practices we audit have their profile filled in at less than 40 %, which directly penalizes their Local Pack ranking.

Google does not favour practice size as such, but group dental organizations often have more resources to generate reviews and maintain multiple location listings. A solo practitioner can outrank a group clinic by optimizing their Google Business Profile more effectively, accumulating more recent reviews, and maintaining better NAP consistency across directories. We work equally well with solo practitioners and multi-provider groups — the method adapts to your structure.

Reviews & Reputation

Our method rests on three ethical pillars that comply with Google's guidelines and the Ordre des dentistes du Quebec standards. First, automated post-visit follow-up by email or SMS with a direct link to your Google listing. Second, training your front-desk staff to naturally mention reviews to satisfied patients — without pressure or selective targeting. Third, systematically responding to existing reviews to demonstrate engagement and improve your conversion rate. Over 6 months, our clients have gone from a small number of reviews to 80 or more.

Never ignore it, and never respond emotionally. Respond within 24 to 48 hours with a professional, empathetic reply: thank the patient for their feedback, express regret that the experience did not meet your standards, and invite them to contact you directly to resolve the matter. A negative review paired with an excellent response often inspires more trust than a perfect record with no critical feedback. We provide response templates tailored to your practice and professional communication guidelines.

There is no magic threshold, but our analysis of Quebec markets shows that in an average Montreal neighbourhood, practices in the top 3 typically have between 50 and 150 reviews with a rating between 4.6 and 5.0. The recency of reviews matters as much as the total volume: a practice with 200 reviews whose most recent dates back 6 months will be disadvantaged compared to a competitor with 60 reviews, 10 of which were posted in the last month. Our system maintains a steady flow of new reviews without additional effort on your part.

Practices with complete Schema Dentist markup, rich descriptions and consistent web mentions are cited considerably more often in these responses. Schema Dentist is a JSON-LD markup standard based on Schema.org that describes your practice in a way that search engines and AI systems can understand. It includes the establishment type (Dentist), your specialties, hours, exact address, languages spoken, appointment booking URL and aggregate rating. It is installed in your website's HTML and is invisible to visitors. Our audits consistently reveal that a significant majority of Quebec dental websites either lack this markup entirely or have it incorrectly implemented.
