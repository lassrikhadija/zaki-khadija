Local Pack & Visibility

Google's Local Pack is the block of 3 results that appears at the top of search results when a patient searches for "osteopath Plateau" or "back pain osteopath Montreal". These 3 results capture up to 75 % of all clicks (BrightLocal 2024). If your clinic is not in that block, you are invisible to the majority of patients searching right now. Google selects clinics for the Local Pack based on relevance, proximity and prominence — three dimensions we optimize specifically for your practice. Appearing in this block is worth considerably more than having a beautiful website with no visibility.

For clinics starting from a low baseline, the first ranking improvements are generally visible after a few weeks following Google Business Profile optimization. Reaching a stable top-3 position in a competitive neighbourhood like Plateau or Rosemont typically requires several months of sustained work. Results depend on competition levels in your neighbourhood, your volume of recent reviews, and the quality of your Schema MedicalBusiness markup. Our audit will give you an honest estimate for your specific situation.

Being on Google Maps is only the first step of a much longer optimization journey. A well-optimized Google Business Profile for an osteopathy clinic includes precise health categories, a complete list of services (back pain, sports injuries, infant osteopathy, prenatal care), recent professional photos, weekly posts, and — most importantly — a steady stream of recent reviews with responses. The majority of clinics we audit have an incompletely filled profile, which directly penalizes their Local Pack ranking.

Neighbourhood pages are dedicated website pages targeting specific geographic searches such as "osteopath Plateau", "osteopath Rosemont", or "osteopath Mile End". Each page contains localized content, long-tail keywords and proximity signals that dominate neighbourhood-level searches. A patient searching for a clinic near their home or office will find your dedicated neighbourhood page, which ranks for that specific local query. These pages are one of the most effective local SEO levers for health professionals in Quebec. You can read more in our complete guide to Google Local Pack for osteopaths →

Reviews & Reputation

Our method rests on three ethical pillars. First, automated post-visit follow-up by email or SMS with a direct link to your Google listing. Second, training your reception staff to mention reviews naturally to satisfied patients — without pressure or selective targeting. Third, systematically responding to existing reviews to demonstrate engagement and improve your click-through rate. Over several months, our clients have gone from a small number of reviews to up to 80 or more (results vary by clinic), while fully complying with professional communication guidelines.

Never ignore it, and never respond emotionally. Respond within 24 to 48 hours with a professional, empathetic reply: thank the patient for their feedback, express regret that the experience did not meet your standards, and invite them to contact you directly to resolve the matter. A negative review paired with an excellent response often inspires more trust than a perfect record with no critical feedback. We provide response templates tailored to your clinic and professional communication guidelines.

There is no magic threshold. Based on our observation of Quebec markets, clinics in the top 3 typically have a substantial volume of recent reviews with a high rating. The recency of reviews matters as much as the total volume: a clinic with many reviews whose most recent dates back 6 months will be disadvantaged compared to a competitor with fewer reviews, several of which were posted in the last month. Our system maintains a steady flow of new reviews without additional effort on your part.

Schema MedicalBusiness is a JSON-LD markup standard based on Schema.org that describes your clinic in a way that search engines and AI systems can understand. It includes the establishment type (MedicalBusiness / Osteopathic), your specialties, hours, exact address, languages spoken, appointment booking URL and aggregate rating. It is installed in your website's HTML and is invisible to visitors. Our audits consistently reveal that a significant majority of Quebec osteopathy websites either lack this markup entirely or have it incorrectly implemented.
