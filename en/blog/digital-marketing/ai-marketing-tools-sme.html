The "Plastic Content" Trap

The biggest risk of 2026 is becoming a business without a soul. If you publish 100% AI-generated text, Google will downrank you (E-E-A-T criteria) and your customers will tire of your impersonal tone.

The "human finish" method:

  1. Generate (AI): Structure and first draft in seconds.
  2. Personalize (Human): Add a real anecdote, a figure from your own experience, or a clear opinion.
  3. Verify (Human): AI can hallucinate. Always fact-check statistics, local prices and cited sources.

The golden rule: 60% AI, 40% Human. That human finish is what protects your SEO and your emotional connection with customers.
