Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from Quebec dentists about online visibility and local SEO.

Why is local SEO especially important for dental clinics in Quebec?

The majority of patients search for a dentist online before making an appointment. According to BrightLocal, 98% of consumers used the internet to find a local business in the past year. A dental clinic that does not appear in Google's local pack loses a considerable share of new patient opportunities to neighbouring competitors who are better positioned.

What is the Google Local Pack and how can a dental clinic appear in it?

The Google Local Pack is the block of three local results that appears at the top of search results for queries like "dentist near me." To appear in it, a clinic must optimize its Google Business Profile (complete information, photos, categories), collect regular patient reviews, and maintain consistent NAP citations across local directories.

How do Google reviews affect a dental clinic's visibility?

According to BrightLocal, 87% of consumers read online reviews for local businesses. Reviews influence both Google's local ranking algorithm and patient trust. Clinics with a high volume of recent, positive reviews are significantly more likely to appear at the top of local search results and receive more appointment requests.

What is a neighbourhood SEO page and why does a dental clinic need one?

A neighbourhood SEO page is a dedicated web page targeting patients in a specific district or borough (for example, "dentist in Plateau-Mont-Royal"). These pages allow a clinic to rank for hyper-local searches, capture patients who search by neighbourhood, and considerably strengthen their presence across their entire service area.

What is Dentist schema markup and how does it help with AI search visibility?

Dentist schema markup is structured data added to a clinic's website that helps search engines and AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) understand key information such as services, hours, location, and accepted insurance. Proper schema implementation significantly improves a clinic's chances of appearing in AI-generated answers and rich results.

What are NAP citations and which Quebec directories matter most for dentists?

NAP citations are consistent mentions of a clinic's Name, Address, and Phone number across online directories. For Quebec dental clinics, the most important directories include Google Business Profile, Yelp, Pages Jaunes (Yellow Pages Canada), the OdQ (Ordre des dentistes du Québec) directory, Healthgrades, and local chamber of commerce listings. Inconsistent NAP data can negatively affect local rankings.
