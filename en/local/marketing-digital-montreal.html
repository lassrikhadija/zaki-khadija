Digital marketing in Montreal: your questions

Digital marketing includes all online actions to attract and convert clients: SEO, Google Ads, social media, email marketing, content marketing and automation. For a Montreal SME, it enables competing with larger companies at a lower cost.

For a Montreal SME, a starting budget of $1,000 to $3,000 per month delivers concrete results. Google Ads starts at $500/month, SEO between $800 and $2,000/month. The key is allocating budget to the most profitable channels for your industry.

Both are complementary. Google Ads generates immediate leads but stops when the budget does. SEO takes 3 to 6 months but generates free, sustainable traffic. For a Montreal SME wanting both quick and lasting results, a combination of both is recommended.

Key KPIs for a Montreal SME: cost per lead (CPL), conversion rate, return on ad spend (ROAS), organic traffic and number of quote requests. NEXTIWEB sets up Google Analytics and GTM to track every conversion and continuously optimize your campaigns.
