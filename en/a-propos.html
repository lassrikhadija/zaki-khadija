I know this situation. I lived it from the inside.

My name is Khadija AitLassri, founder of NEXTIWEB.

My career started in IT — network administration, then web development. But early on, I wanted something beyond employment. In 2015, I became an entrepreneur. I built my own business, managed teams, negotiated contracts, delivered complex projects under pressure. I learned the hard way what it means to depend on your results to make a living.

Then COVID hit. In 2023, I sold my franchise and had to rebuild everything from scratch.

I went back to my roots in IT — and did what many do : I built my own website. Technically solid. Well structured. Clean code. And I waited for visitors.

Zero traffic. Zero inquiries. The site existed — but it was invisible.

That's when I understood that mastering code isn't enough. I decided to find the real answers : I studied e-commerce, online buyer behaviour, SEO, and the impact of artificial intelligence on how people search for and choose their service providers.

What I discovered changed everything.

A site that generates clients isn't just about design or technology. It's the intersection of a structure built to convert, content optimized to be found — on Google and on AI search engines — and a real understanding of what your client feels and looks for before they even call you.

NEXTIWEB was born from that combination. Not from a marketing course. Not from an online program. From the real experience of an entrepreneur who went through failure, understood why, and found the method that actually works.
