Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from Quebec lawyers about online visibility and local SEO.

Why is local SEO especially important for law firms in Quebec?

The majority of prospective clients search for legal help online before contacting a firm. According to BrightLocal, 98% of consumers used the internet to find a local business in the past year. A law firm that does not appear in Google's local pack loses a considerable share of new client inquiries to competing firms that are better positioned.

What is the Google Local Pack and how can a law firm appear in it?

The Google Local Pack is the block of three local results that appears at the top of search results for queries like "lawyer near me" or "divorce lawyer Montreal." To appear in it, a law firm must optimize its Google Business Profile, collect regular client reviews, and maintain consistent NAP citations across legal directories.

How do Google reviews affect a law firm's visibility while respecting Bar rules?

According to BrightLocal, 87% of consumers read online reviews for local businesses. Reviews significantly influence local rankings and client trust. Law firms can collect reviews ethically and in compliance with Barreau du Québec rules by requesting feedback without disclosing case details and by responding professionally without confirming any lawyer-client relationship.

What is a specialty or neighbourhood page for lawyer SEO?

A specialty or neighbourhood page is a dedicated page targeting a specific practice area and geography (for example, "divorce lawyer Rosemont" or "immigration lawyer Montreal"). These pages allow a firm to rank for hyper-local, high-intent searches and considerably expand their geographic and practice-area reach without launching a new site.

What is LegalService schema markup and how does it help with AI search visibility?

LegalService schema markup is structured data added to a law firm's website that helps search engines and AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) understand the firm's practice areas, hours, location, and credentials. Proper schema implementation significantly improves a firm's chances of appearing in AI-generated answers and rich results.

What are NAP citations and which Quebec legal directories matter most?

NAP citations are consistent mentions of a firm's Name, Address, and Phone number across online directories. For Quebec law firms, the most important directories include Google Business Profile, the Barreau du Québec directory, Yelp, Yellow Pages Canada, Justia, and local chamber of commerce listings. Inconsistent NAP data can negatively affect local rankings.
