for ChatGPT, Perplexity & Google AI Overviews

What Is GEO? (Generative Engine Optimization)

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your content to be cited, recommended, and quoted by AI-powered search engines. While traditional SEO focuses on ranking in the 10 blue links, GEO focuses on being the source that AI systems choose when composing their answers.

🔍

Traditional SEO

Rank in Google's organic results. Users click your link and visit your site.

🤖

GEO

Be cited in AI answers. Your brand and expertise appear in ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overviews.

🎯

AEO

Answer Engine Optimization — optimizing for featured snippets and voice search answers.

Key insight: GEO does not replace traditional SEO — it builds on it. The sites that AI systems cite most are those that already have strong E-E-A-T signals, good technical SEO, and authoritative backlinks. Fix your SEO foundation first, then layer GEO tactics on top.

How AI Systems Select Sources

AI search engines use different selection criteria than Google's organic ranking algorithm. Understanding these criteria is the foundation of GEO:

  • Factual precision: AI models favor content with specific, verifiable facts, statistics, and named sources rather than vague generalizations.
  • Clear question-answer format: Content structured as explicit questions and direct answers is much easier for AI to parse and cite.
  • Structured data (schema): FAQ schema, HowTo schema, and Article schema make your content machine-readable — AI systems can directly extract structured answers.
  • E-E-A-T signals: AI models are trained to prefer credible, authoritative sources. Named authors, citations, and trust signals all improve your citation rate.
  • Topical depth: A site with comprehensive coverage of a topic cluster is cited more often than one with a single isolated article on that topic.

7 GEO Tactics to Apply Today

1

Add FAQ Schema to Every Informational Page

FAQ schema is the single most impactful GEO tactic. It presents your content as explicit question-answer pairs that AI systems can directly extract. Every article should include 5–8 FAQs with specific, complete answers. This also increases your Google AI Overviews eligibility.

2

Use Statistics With Named Sources

Replace "many businesses find that…" with "According to Backlinko's 2024 analysis of 11.8 million results, pages in position 1 have 3.8× more backlinks than positions 2–10." AI systems cite content with verifiable, specific data far more often than generic claims.

3

Structure Content as Direct Question-Answer Pairs

Use H2 headings phrased as questions: "How does Google AI Overviews choose its sources?" Then open the section with a direct, complete answer in the first 2 sentences. AI systems read the heading as the question and the opening paragraph as the answer.

4

Build Topical Depth With Content Clusters

A site with 10 interlinked articles on "SEO for restaurants" will be cited far more often than a site with one generic SEO article. AI systems favor sources that demonstrate comprehensive expertise. See our guide on topic clusters and pillar pages.

5

Keep Content Fresh With Explicit Update Dates

AI systems prioritize recent, up-to-date information. Add a "Last updated: [date]" marker to your articles and update the dateModified in your Article schema. Review and refresh your top pages every 6–12 months.

6

Optimize robots.txt Thoughtfully

Decide which AI crawlers you want to allow. Blocking them prevents citations but also prevents training data usage. Most businesses benefit from allowing citations:

# Allow major search engines
User-agent: Googlebot
Allow: /

# Allow AI citation crawlers (recommended for most sites)
User-agent: GPTBot
Allow: /

User-agent: PerplexityBot
Allow: /

User-agent: ClaudeBot
Allow: /

# Block Google training data (separate from AI Overviews)
User-agent: Google-Extended
Disallow: /
7

Monitor Your AI Visibility

Regularly query ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews for your target keywords. Track whether your brand or site appears. Use Search Console to monitor AI Overviews impressions for your top pages. Set up Google Alerts for your brand name to catch citations.
